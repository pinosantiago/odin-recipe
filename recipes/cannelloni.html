Cannelloni

Description

Cannelloni is a recipe of Italian origin. They are very versatile since they can be filled with different ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1kg lean minced beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 x 660g jars passata with basil
  • large pinch caster sugar
  • 400g dried cannelloni tubes

For the topping

  1. 50g butter
  2. 50g plain flour
  3. 600ml whole milk
  4. 140g soft cheese with garlic and herbs
  5. 140g parmesan, grated
